Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this page is to have information about Whitewater Adventures more accesible than ever. We also wanted to create an easy platform where anyone can come to and make a reservation or book a tour without ever having to pick up the phone. This site is going to help Whitewater Adventures get our name out there so everyone can see that we are the best whitewater rafting company around. We hope to see you soon!

Audience

Whitewater Adventure's target audience is anyone 18 years of age or older who has an adventurous side and is a self-proclaimed adrenaline junkie!
What do our customers want?
-They want to make everlasting memories with friends and family while rafting down some of the most beautiful rivers and landscapes in the country.
What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?
-The expect to find pricing, locations and all of the activities and tours we offer, as well as the dates of when our tours/activities take place.
How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)?
-I believe our customers will mainly access our site through their mobile device because it would be the most convenient. I also think there will be some that will check out our site on their computer or laptop.
