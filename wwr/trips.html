Our best selling trip, The Grand Canyon Adventure, truly lives up to its name. This exhilarating and unforgettable adventure combines the thrill of navigating huge rapis with the jaw dropping beauty of one of the world's most iconic natural wonders. As you set out on this adventure with your guide and group, you will have your breath taken away from the towering canyon walls that stretch up into the sky over a thousand feet. Throughout this adventure, you will encounter a series of rapids that range from gentle to heart-pounding drops!

The Colorado River expedition is an expedition for the entire family! You start out in Fort Collins, CO as you start down the incredibly beautiful Colorado River. When you combine gentle rapids and giant wakes with the gorgeous scenery of Colorado and all the wildlife you will see along the way, that is what makes this a truly an unforgettable experience that you and your family will remember and cherish forever!

Welcome to the beautiful Salmon River in Idaho! This particular trip is not for the weak of heart as it offers an exhilarating experience through some of the most rugged and pristine wilderness in the whole United Sates. The Salmon River, also known as "the River of No Return," winds its way through deep canyons, dense forests, and breathtaking mountain scenery. As you raft down this incredible river you will be provided with a thrilling and unforgettable experience for the highest level of adventure you could ever want.

Right outside of Yosemite in California, lies the exhilarating Tuolumne River Rapids trip. Take part in an exhilarating and scenic expedition through the heart of California's Sierra Nevada Mountains. Known for its stunning granite cliffs, lush forests, and crystal-clear waters, the Tuolumne River provides an unforgettable experience for rafters of all levels. As you navigate these rapids you will truly feel the adrenaline and you plunge through the churing whitewater with your expert guide safely leading the way. Between rapids you will be able to realx a bit and soak in the beauty of the scenery that is surrounding you.
